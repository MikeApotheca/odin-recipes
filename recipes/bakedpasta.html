Baked pasta (Brian Lagerstrom's recipe)

Baked pasta is a delicious and easy dish that can be made with only a few ingredients and just a little bit of time. It is easy to make and fast. Ingredient list below. You must saute the garlic in olive oil, brown the sausage, and add in the onions. The you add in the tomato paste and spahetti and crushed tomates.

After all the liquids are in , add the spaghetti and bake for 15 mins , then add cheese.

Ingredients

  • garlic
  • olive oil
  • crushed tomatos
  • Italian spices
  • Italian sausage
