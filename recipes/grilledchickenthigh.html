Section for grilled chicken thigh sandwich

This is the body of the recipe

Flatten out chicken thighs and score them to get them flatter. Add to a bowl with mayo and Sazon seasoning and mix to coat thoruoughly. Preheat grill to medium high and grill for 12 minutes, flipping every 3 minutes. Let rest then add to bun with one slice of American cheese and ketchup.

Ingredients list

  • Chicken thighs
  • mayo
  • Sazon seasoning
  • Hamburger buns
  • American cheese slices
